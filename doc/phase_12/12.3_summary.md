## Strategies Implemented

### 1. Bollinger Bands Reversion Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/mean_reversion/bollinger_bands.rs`
- **Enhancement**: Added RSI confirmation filter to avoid catching falling knives
- **Key Features**:
  - Bollinger Bands (period 20, 2 std dev)
  - RSI filter (period 14, oversold <30, overbought >70)
  - Entry: Price crosses below lower band AND RSI oversold
  - Exit: Middle band crossover, RSI overbought, or TP/SL
- **Tests**: 3 unit tests
- **Lines of Code**: ~295

### 2. RSI Reversion Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/mean_reversion/rsi_reversion.rs`
- **Key Features**:
  - Pure RSI-based mean reversion (period 14)
  - Optional 200-period SMA trend filter
  - Entry: RSI <= 30 (oversold) AND price > 200 SMA
  - Exit: RSI >= 50 (neutral) or >= 70 (overbought)
- **Tests**: 4 unit tests
- **Lines of Code**: ~320

### 3. Stochastic Reversion Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/mean_reversion/stoch_reversion.rs`
- **Key Features**:
  - Stochastic oscillator (%K=14, %D=3, smooth=3)
  - Oversold/overbought thresholds (20/80)
  - Entry: %K <= 20 (oversold)
  - Exit: %K >= 80 (overbought) or bearish crossover
- **Tests**: 3 unit tests
- **Lines of Code**: ~325
- **Note**: Added Stochastic indicator to indicators.rs

### 4. Z-Score Reversion Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/mean_reversion/zscore_reversion.rs`
- **Key Features**:
  - Statistical z-score calculation (20-period lookback)
  - Entry: z-score <= -2.0 (2 std dev below mean)
  - Exit: z-score >= 0.0 (return to mean)
  - Minimum 1% price change filter to avoid flat markets
- **Tests**: 4 unit tests
- **Lines of Code**: ~350

### 5. Price Channel (Donchian) Reversion Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/mean_reversion/price_channel.rs`
- **Key Features**:
  - Donchian channels (20-period highest/lowest)
  - Entry: Price <= lowest low
  - Exit: Price >= middle of channel (50%) or highest high
- **Tests**: 4 unit tests
- **Lines of Code**: ~310

### 6. Keltner Channel Reversion Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/mean_reversion/keltner_reversion.rs`
- **Key Features**:
  - Keltner channels (EMA 20 + ATR 10 * 2.0)
  - Volume confirmation (1.5x average volume)
  - Entry: Price <= lower band AND high volume
  - Exit: Price >= middle band (EMA) or upper band
- **Tests**: 3 unit tests
- **Lines of Code**: ~315

### 7. Statistical Arbitrage Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/mean_reversion/stat_arb.rs`
- **Key Features**:
  - Adapted for spot-only trading (z-score based)
  - 30-period lookback for mean/std dev
  - Entry: z-score <= -2.0
  - Exit: z-score >= 0.0 (return to mean)
- **Tests**: 4 unit tests
- **Lines of Code**: ~335
- **Note**: Full pairs trading would require dual-asset correlation
