## Strategies Implemented

### 1. RSI Momentum Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/momentum/rsi_momentum.rs`
- **Hypothesis**: `doc/phase_12/hypotheses/momentum/rsi_momentum.md`
- **Logic**: Enters when RSI crosses above 50 (momentum threshold), exits when RSI falls below 50
- **Key Features**: 
  - Signal strength based on RSI level (1.0 if RSI ≥ 60, else 0.7)
  - 5% take profit, 3% stop loss
  - Distinguishes from RSI mean reversion by following momentum vs fading extremes

### 2. MACD Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/momentum/macd_strategy.rs`
- **Hypothesis**: `doc/phase_12/hypotheses/momentum/macd_strategy.md`
- **Logic**: Enters when MACD crosses above signal line AND price > EMA(50)
- **Key Features**:
  - Dual confirmation: MACD crossover + trend filter
  - 5% take profit, 5% stop loss
  - Exits on MACD bearish crossover or price crosses below EMA

### 3. ROC (Rate of Change) Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/momentum/roc_strategy.rs`
- **Hypothesis**: `doc/phase_12/hypotheses/momentum/roc_strategy.md`
- **Logic**: Enters when ROC crosses above +2%, exits when ROC crosses below -1%
- **Key Features**:
  - Measures price momentum as percentage change
  - 5% take profit, 3% stop loss
  - Detects accelerating/decelerating momentum

### 4. ADX Trend Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/momentum/adx_trend.rs`
- **Hypothesis**: `doc/phase_12/hypotheses/momentum/adx_trend.md`
- **Logic**: Enters when ADX crosses above 25 (strong trend) in uptrend
- **Key Features**:
  - Filters entries by trend strength (ADX > 25)
  - Exits when ADX falls below 20 (weak trend)
  - Trend direction detection to avoid counter-trend entries
  - 5% take profit, 3% stop loss

### 5. Momentum Factor Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/momentum/momentum_factor.rs`
- **Hypothesis**: `doc/phase_12/hypotheses/momentum/momentum_factor.md`
- **Logic**: Combines 3 factors (price momentum, volume momentum, RSI > 50), requires 2/3 positive
- **Key Features**:
  - Multi-factor approach reduces false signals
  - Configurable minimum factors threshold
  - 5% take profit, 3% stop loss
  - Signal strength based on number of positive factors

### 6. Volume Momentum Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/momentum/volume_momentum.rs`
- **Hypothesis**: `doc/phase_12/hypotheses/momentum/volume_momentum.md`
- **Logic**: Enters when price crosses above EMA AND volume ≥ 1.5x average
- **Key Features**:
  - Volume confirmation of price momentum
  - Detects volume surge and increasing volume
  - 5% take profit, 3% stop loss
  - Exits on volume decline or price crosses below EMA

### 7. Multi-Timeframe Momentum Strategy
- **File**: `crates/strategy/src/strategies/momentum/multi_tf_momentum.rs`
- **Hypothesis**: `doc/phase_12/hypotheses/momentum/multi_tf_momentum.md`
- **Logic**: Requires alignment: Price > Fast EMA(20) > Slow EMA(50)
- **Key Features**:
  - Multi-timeframe alignment filter
  - Two entry types: price crosses above fast EMA, or fast crosses above slow
  - 5% take profit, 3% stop loss
  - Exits when alignment breaks
